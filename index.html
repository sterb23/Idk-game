ZOMBIE SHOOTER

[HOW TO PLAY]

Walk around and shoot the zombies. Pick up ammo from the floor so you don't run out.

COMPUTER CONTROLS:
• Move: W / S or Up/Down arrows
• Look around: A / D or Left/Right arrows
• Shoot: SPACEBAR

PHONE CONTROLS:
• Left circle to move and look
• Right red button to shoot

GAME OVER

The zombies got you.
